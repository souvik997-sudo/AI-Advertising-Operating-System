# 1. Introduction

## What is Quality Control?

Quality Control defines the process of reviewing, validating, and improving AI-generated outputs before they are delivered to the user.

Its purpose is to ensure that every response meets the required standards of accuracy, consistency, completeness, readability, and implementation readiness.

Quality Control focuses on evaluating the quality of the final output rather than generating new information.

---

## Why Quality Control Matters

Even well-structured outputs may contain factual inaccuracies, inconsistencies, missing information, or implementation issues.

A systematic quality control process helps identify and correct these problems before the response is delivered.

This improves reliability, professionalism, and user confidence.

---

## Purpose of This Module

The purpose of this module is to establish a standardized quality assurance process for evaluating AI-generated outputs.

It provides validation principles, review methods, and quality standards that help ensure every response is complete, accurate, and suitable for real-world use.
