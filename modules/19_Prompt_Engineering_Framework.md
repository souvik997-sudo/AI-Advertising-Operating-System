# Introduction

The Prompt Engineering Framework defines the standardized methodology for designing, structuring, and optimizing AI prompts within the AI Advertising Operating System (AAOS).

Its responsibility is to transform validated business requirements, marketing objectives, and framework outputs into clear, reusable, and model-agnostic prompts that consistently produce high-quality AI-generated results.

This framework provides a structured approach for creating reliable, scalable, and task-oriented prompts across different AI models, marketing workflows, business scenarios, and automation systems.

# Purpose

This module defines how the AI should design, structure, and optimize prompts that consistently generate accurate, reliable, and context-aware outputs across different AI models.

The objective is to transform validated business requirements, marketing objectives, and framework knowledge into reusable prompts that improve output quality, consistency, and execution efficiency.

The AI should produce clear, structured, model-agnostic prompts that support scalable marketing workflows, business automation, and high-quality content generation.

# Prompt Engineering Philosophy

Effective prompt engineering begins with understanding the business objective, the required task, and the desired output.

Every prompt should communicate clear instructions, sufficient context, and well-defined expectations to maximize the quality and consistency of AI-generated results.

The AI should prioritize clarity, accuracy, context, structure, and reusability over unnecessary prompt complexity.

Well-designed prompts should minimize ambiguity, improve reasoning, support consistent execution, and produce reliable outputs while remaining adaptable across different AI models, marketing workflows, and business scenarios.

# Prompt Structure

A well-designed prompt should guide the AI through a clear, structured, and logical sequence that minimizes ambiguity and maximizes output quality.

The following components form the standard prompt structure used throughout AAOS.

---

## Objective

Clearly define what the AI is expected to accomplish.

The objective should describe the primary task without unnecessary complexity.

---

## Context

Provide all relevant business, marketing, technical, or creative information required to perform the task accurately.

The context should reduce assumptions and improve output relevance.

---

## Role

Assign an appropriate role that aligns with the required expertise and expected perspective.

The role should support better reasoning and domain-specific responses.

---

## Instructions

Provide clear, specific, and actionable instructions.

The AI should understand exactly how the task should be executed.

---

## Constraints

Define limitations, requirements, formatting rules, and boundaries that the AI must follow.

Constraints should improve consistency and reduce undesired outputs.

---

## Expected Output

Clearly specify the desired output format, structure, level of detail, and quality expectations.

The expected output should be measurable and easy to validate.

# Prompt Optimization Rules

The AI should optimize every prompt to maximize clarity, consistency, efficiency, and output quality.

The AI should:

- Clearly define the business objective before writing the prompt.
- Remove ambiguity and unnecessary instructions.
- Provide sufficient context for accurate reasoning.
- Organize instructions in a logical sequence.
- Use clear, precise, and action-oriented language.
- Specify the expected output format and level of detail.
- Eliminate redundant information.
- Maintain consistency with the applicable AAOS frameworks.
- Balance prompt completeness with readability.
- Review and refine the prompt before execution.

---

# Prompt Adaptation Rules

The AI should adapt prompts based on the task, business context, target AI model, and expected output while preserving the original objective.

The AI should:

- Adapt prompts to the business domain.
- Adjust prompts for different marketing objectives.
- Modify prompts for various content formats.
- Adapt prompts to different communication styles when required.
- Preserve brand consistency across prompt variations.
- Optimize prompts for different AI capabilities without changing the intended outcome.
- Maintain logical structure across all prompt adaptations.
- Ensure adapted prompts remain clear, reusable, and scalable.
- Validate prompt effectiveness after adaptation.
- Preserve the original business objective throughout the adaptation process.
