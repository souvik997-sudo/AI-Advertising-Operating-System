# Prompt Templates

## Version

**v1.0**

---

# Introduction

The Prompt Templates module provides a standardized collection of reusable prompt templates within the AI Advertising Operating System (AAOS).

Its responsibility is to transform the Prompt Engineering Framework into practical, execution-ready templates that enable consistent AI interactions across different business requirements, marketing objectives, and automation workflows.

This module establishes a reusable prompt library that allows the AI to efficiently generate standardized prompts while maintaining quality, consistency, scalability, and alignment with the overall AAOS architecture.

---

# Purpose

This module defines how the AI should organize, select, customize, and execute reusable prompt templates for different business requirements, marketing objectives, and operational workflows.

The objective is to provide standardized prompt templates that improve execution efficiency, maintain output consistency, reduce prompt creation time, and support scalable AI-driven marketing operations.

The AI should intelligently select the most appropriate prompt template, adapt it to the specific business context, and generate high-quality outputs while maintaining consistency with the AAOS frameworks and standards.

---

# Template Philosophy

Effective prompt templates begin with understanding the business objective, the required task, and the expected outcome.

Every template should provide a standardized foundation that enables consistent, efficient, and high-quality AI execution while remaining flexible enough to adapt to different business scenarios.

The AI should prioritize reusability, clarity, contextual accuracy, and framework consistency over creating new prompts for repetitive tasks.

Well-designed prompt templates should reduce prompt engineering effort, improve execution reliability, support automation, and enable scalable AI-assisted marketing workflows across different industries, platforms, and AI models.

# Template Organization

The Prompt Templates module organizes reusable prompt templates into standardized categories based on business objectives, marketing functions, and operational workflows.

Each template category is designed to address a specific business need while maintaining consistency with the Prompt Engineering Framework and the overall AAOS architecture.

The AI should identify the appropriate template category before selecting, customizing, and executing a prompt to ensure accurate, efficient, and reliable results.

# Template Usage Guidelines

The AI should apply prompt templates consistently while adapting them to the specific business context, marketing objective, and operational requirements.

The AI should:

- Select the most appropriate template category before execution.
- Understand the business objective before customizing any template.
- Replace placeholder information with validated business data.
- Preserve the overall structure of the original template.
- Adapt templates without changing their intended purpose.
- Maintain consistency with applicable AAOS frameworks and standards.
- Ensure prompts remain clear, reusable, and context-aware.
- Validate the customized prompt before execution.
- Optimize the prompt when necessary while preserving template integrity.
- Review the final output to ensure the marketing objective has been achieved.
